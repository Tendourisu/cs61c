Contact Us

There are a couple ways you can contact us:

  • Private Piazza post: These can be seen by all instructors and TAs. Please do not make private posts to individual staff members, as they're likely to be missed.
  • [email protected]: Emails sent here can only be seen by instructors and head TAs. Future instructors and head TAs will also be able to read emails here, but we can delete emails upon request.
  • Individual email addresses: Email addresses for individual staff members are listed on the staff page. We encourage you to use the options above if they meet your needs, since they will reach more people.
